Here's your plan to start a gym in the next 30 days:

---

**1. Priority Order:**

1.  **Define Needs (Days 1-2):** What are your goals? Budget? Ideal location (distance)?
2.  **Research & Shortlist (Days 3-7):** Find 3-5 gyms online (Google Maps, reviews). Call for info/trial offers.
3.  **Visit & Decide (Days 8-14):** Visit your top 2-3 choices. Take a trial. Make a decision.
4.  **Sign Up & Prepare (Days 15-21):** Enroll in chosen gym. Pack your gym bag.
5.  **First Sessions (Days 22-30):** Go to the gym 2-3 times. Focus on consistency.

---

**2. Daily Plan (Initial 2 weeks, then routine building):**

*   **Days 1-3:**
    *   **Action:** List 3-5 personal goals (e.g., strength, cardio, stress relief). Set a realistic budget. Open Google Maps, search "gyms near me," save 5-7 potentials.
*   **Days 4-7:**
    *   **Action:** Check gym websites for hours, amenities, and pricing. Call your top 3 for trial details. Schedule 1-2 visits.
*   **Days 8-10:**
    *   **Action:** Visit 1-2 gyms. Ask questions. Consider pros/cons (location, equipment, atmosphere, cost). Make your decision.
*   **Days 11-14:**
    *   **Action:** Sign up for membership. Download their app if available. Plan your first workout (even if it's just 30 mins cardio). Lay out gym clothes.
*   **Days 15-30:**
    *   **Action:** Aim for 2-3 gym sessions per week. Focus on getting there consistently. Explore different machines/classes if comfortable.

---

**3. Productivity Tips:**

*   **Set a Hard Deadline:** Pick a specific "sign-up by" date on your calendar.
*   **Prepare the Night Before:** Lay out clothes, pack your bag. Less friction = more likely to go.
*   **Start Small:** Don't aim for an hour-long session every day. 20-30 minutes, 2-3 times a week is a fantastic start.
*   **Accountability:** Tell a friend or family member about your gym goal.
*   **Track Progress:** Log your visits. Seeing consistency builds momentum.
