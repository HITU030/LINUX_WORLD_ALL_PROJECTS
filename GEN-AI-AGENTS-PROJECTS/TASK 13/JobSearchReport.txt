As an AI Career Advisor, I'm happy to help you with your career goals.

**Suitable Job Roles:**

Based on your skills and interests, here are some suitable job roles for an AI Engineer:

1. **Machine Learning Engineer**: Develop and deploy machine learning models using Python, SQL, and Gen-AI.
2. **Data Scientist (AI Focus)**: Work with data to develop predictive models and insights using SQL, Python, and Power BI.
3. **Natural Language Processing (NLP) Engineer**: Design and develop NLP models and applications using Python, SQL, and Gen-AI.
4. **Conversational AI Engineer**: Build conversational interfaces using prompt engineering and Gen-AI.
5. **AI/ML Consultant**: Work with clients to identify business opportunities and develop data-driven solutions using AI and ML techniques.

**Required Skills To Improve:**

To take your career to the next level, focus on developing these skills:

1. **Cloud Computing**: Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, or Google Cloud Platform (GCP) experience.
2. **Deep Learning**: Knowledge of deep learning frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Keras.
3. **Data Visualization**: Expertise in data visualization tools like Tableau, Power BI, or D3.js.
4. **Business Acumen**: Understand the business aspects of AI and ML, including project management, budgeting, and sales.
5. **Communication Skills**: Develop strong communication skills to effectively convey technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.

**Career Roadmap:**

Here's a suggested career roadmap:

1. **0-2 years:** Focus on building a strong foundation in Python, SQL, Power BI, and Gen-AI. Start working on small projects and contribute to open-source repositories.
2. **2-5 years:** Gain experience in machine learning and deep learning using popular frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch. Work on more complex projects and participate in hackathons.
3. **5-10 years:** Specialize in a specific area of AI/ML, such as NLP or computer vision. Develop expertise in cloud computing and data visualization tools.
4. **10+ years:** Take on leadership roles, mentor junior engineers, and work on high-profile projects. Continuously update your skills to stay current with industry trends.

**Interview Preparation Tips:**

To prepare for AI-related interviews:

1. Review common interview questions: Practice answering behavioral, technical, and whiteboard-style questions.
2. Develop a strong portfolio: Showcase your projects and achievements on platforms like GitHub or GitLab.
3. Familiarize yourself with industry tools: Brush up on popular AI/ML frameworks, libraries, and technologies.
4. Prepare case studies: Think about real-world scenarios where you've applied AI/ML concepts to solve problems.
5. Stay updated: Follow industry blogs, attend webinars, and participate in online forums to stay current with the latest trends.

Remember, career growth is a continuous process. Focus on building your skills, networking, and staying adaptable to industry changes. Good luck!
